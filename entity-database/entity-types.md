# Entity Types

The [DropThe entity database](https://dropthe.org) organizes data across eight primary categories.

## Companies

Technology firms, studios, publishers, and corporations tracked with structured business data.

Browse: [dropthe.org/companies/](https://dropthe.org/companies/)

## People

Executives, creators, developers, and public figures connected to tracked entities.

Browse: [dropthe.org/people/](https://dropthe.org/people/)

## Games

Video games across all platforms with studio, publisher, and franchise connections.

Browse: [dropthe.org/games/](https://dropthe.org/games/)

## Movies

Films tracked with cast, crew, studio, and streaming availability data.

Browse: [dropthe.org/movies/](https://dropthe.org/movies/)

## Series

TV shows and streaming series with episode data and platform availability.

Browse: [dropthe.org/series/](https://dropthe.org/series/)

## Crypto

Cryptocurrency tokens and blockchain projects with market and project data.

Browse: [dropthe.org/crypto/](https://dropthe.org/crypto/)

## Brands

Consumer brands, product lines, and trademark holders.

Browse: [dropthe.org/brands/](https://dropthe.org/brands/)

## Franchises

Cross-media franchises spanning games, movies, series, and merchandise.

Browse: [dropthe.org/franchises/](https://dropthe.org/franchises/)
